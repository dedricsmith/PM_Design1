2023 Annual Report

2022 has been quite a busy year! Thanks to evidence-based prevention and harm reduction strategies across the state, we are beginning to see a decrease in overdose deaths from the last year. While there is still a lot of work to be done, this is an encouraging sign.

With that, overall, substance misuse continues to be a large concern with alcohol-related deaths rising, an increased positivity rate in workforce drug testing (bringing Michigan higher than the national average), and new legislation proposed on a regular basis that would increase access to substances in Michigan.

See our 2022 Annual report, detailing accomplishments over the last year, updates on the strategic plan, and goals for 2023. Prevention Network is proud to support you in your prevention activities!

About Us

Prevention Network is a statewide not-for-profit that aims to cultivate healthy communities across Michigan by providing substance misuse prevention resources, technical assistance, and support to all 83 Michigan counties. We strive to bridge the gap between research and practice for the enhanced effectiveness of local prevention efforts.

We are comprehensive in our makeup, embracing a variety of viewpoints and methods of approach in dealing with substance misuse prevention and related problems. We serve a diverse set of community groups, professional and volunteer, whose autonomy we respect. We provide support through which their effectiveness can be increased and common goals can be identified and achieved.
